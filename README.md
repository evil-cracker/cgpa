# CGPA-calculator-for-mechanical-engineering-
First select the subject name and select the grade and credit . if your subject is not present in the list then select the other in the subject box. and the click the enter button and repeat the process . After finishing entering the details just click calculate button , it will tell you the CGPA or SGPA of your subjects. 
If you enter wrong detatils then see the serial number of the detail and then click the change button and enter the serial number and enter the correct details and click the ok button 
Then press back button , in the home page you will see nothing until you press the update button
After clicking the update button , click the calculate the button.
 It will calculate and tell you the answer.
